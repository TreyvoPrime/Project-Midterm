STORY:
The story starts with the coronation of Napoleon Bonaparte. You must guide your way through an adventure as a leader and lead your country to greatness. Defeat is possible make the correct moves to achieve a good ending.
ENDINGS-------
there are 8 total endings. The following listed:
1. Victory Ending
2. Assasination Ending
3. Burning Moscow Ending
4. Defeat Ending
5. Good leader Ending
6. Naval Supremacy Ending
7. Paradise Ending
8. Real Ending
--------------------------------------------
At the battle of Waterloo you should issue the commands correctly in a corresponding order. Press the button for the command to be activated. 
HINT: Fire button is the last button pressed
